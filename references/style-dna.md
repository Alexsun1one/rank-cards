# Rank Cards Style DNA

Use this reference before final generation. A rank card's identity comes from unity and
authority: every subject treated identically, a stated basis, bounded color-coded tiers, and
type that survives a thumbnail.

## Core Look

- Default 3:4 vertical (1080×1440) for 视频号/抖音/小红书 covers; 1:1 for grid carousels; 16:9
  for YouTube thumbnails.
- One unified portrait pipeline across all subjects (see `portrait-pipeline.md`).
- 3–6 tiers, parallel-form labels, top tier scarce, warm→cool color ramp.
- The basis line and scope printed on the card — small but present.
- One focal structure (podium/versus/pyramid/single-hero) on covers; the eye lands somewhere
  first.
- Restrained, consistent saturation; the tier ramp carries the color, not random rainbow fills.
- Generous spacing; names and numbers never crammed.

## Typography Hierarchy

Largest/loudest → smallest:

1. Title / headline — the hook (`历史最强后卫排名` / `NBA GOAT 段位` / `他到底什么水平?`).
2. Tier label — per band, bold, in a colored cap; the anchor of each tier.
3. Rank number — oversized numeral for ranked layouts; often the hero graphic.
4. Subject name — same baseline and size for every subject; legibility is non-negotiable.
5. Basis / scope line — the credibility subtitle; small but present.
6. Stat chips (optional) — small pills (`金球×8` `MVP×5`); 2–4 max per subject.
7. Source / disclaimer — tiny, in a corner (`数据来源` / `个人向`).

Outline or duotone-fill big numerals so they read on busy portraits.

## Components

- Medals/crown for top ranks; FIFA-style corner badge (rating + position glyph) for
  single-hero and grid.
- Tier dividers: clear rules or colored bands; each tier gets its accent color.
- Uniform portrait ring; a tier-colored ring double-encodes tier membership.

## Not a Collage

- no mismatched crops, backgrounds, lighting, or art styles
- no leftover original photo backgrounds behind cut-out subjects
- no oil painting next to a modern photo next to a cartoon
- no one soft, low-res, or watermarked face among sharp ones

## Not a Default Tier-Maker

- no bare gray rows with clip-art
- no inconsistent saturation across bands
- no mixed-form tier labels (S / 天王 / 二流 together)
- no 7+ tier sprawl that shrinks portraits to noise

## World-Class Quality Bar

Accept only if:

- every portrait plausibly came from one studio on one day (unity gate)
- the basis and scope are printed and defensible (credibility gate)
- tiers are bounded, parallel, color-ramped, and scarce at the top
- the title and all names are readable at a 2cm thumbnail
- there is one clear focal structure on a cover

Reject if:

- it reads as a collage of scraped photos
- the ranking has no stated basis or scope
- tiers sprawl or labels mix forms
- names or numbers are unreadable
- it looks like a bare default tier-maker
